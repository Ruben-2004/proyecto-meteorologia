# 📈 Results

## Overview

The results confirm the presence of the Urban Heat Island effect in all analysed cities, with varying intensity depending on environmental and urban factors.

---

## 🌡️ UHI Patterns

- UHI is consistently positive
- Stronger during nighttime
- Seasonal variability observed

---

## 🌍 Influence of Land Use

- Urban areas significantly increase UHI
- Vegetation reduces temperature differences
- Mixed land use leads to intermediate effects

---

## 📊 Model Results

### Linear Regression

- Positive coefficients for urban variables
- Negative coefficients for vegetation

### Ridge and Lasso Regression

- Similar trends with improved stability
- Reduced sensitivity to small sample size

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

- Small number of cities
- Limited spatial resolution
- Potential measurement errors in stations

---

## 🚀 Future Work

- Incorporate satellite data (e.g., land surface temperature)
- Increase number of cities
- Explore non-linear models
- Analyse temporal dynamics in more detail
